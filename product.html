    The perfect balance between warmth and freshness. The mandarin opens bright and citrusy, then slowly melts into the richness of tobacco for a deep, elegant finish. It feels luxurious, long-lasting, and incredibly sophisticated. Definitely a fragrance that stands out in any collection.

    Solana Canabal ★ ★ ★ ★

    September 26, 2025

    A very refined and unique fragrance. The mandarin gives it a fresh spark at first, but the tobacco dominates as it settles, making it quite heavy. Beautifully crafted, but I’d recommend it more for evenings or colder weather. A solid choice, though not my everyday scent.
